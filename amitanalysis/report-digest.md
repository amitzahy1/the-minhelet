## Defects (why it's a prior, not gospel)
1. **Phantom teams in the tail.** The champion table's ranks 18-48 list teams **not in the actual draw** (Denmark, Poland, Ukraine, Cameroon, Costa Rica, Wales, Serbia, Honduras, Indonesia) — stale/hallucinated. Only the top-team rows + the Chapter-4 group analysis (which uses the *correct* draw) are reliable.
2. **Bracket assumption differs from the app.** The report puts Spain & France in the same half (semi-final meeting); the app's bracket (which scores the game) puts them in opposite halves. Use the app's structure for your bets.
3. AI-generated prose can over-narrate; quantitative tables were preferred over claims, and all cross-checked vs the live market.
